Inquire

Begin
a conversation.

We accept a limited number of new commissions each year. If your project interests us — and if our approach interests you — we will arrange a call within three working days of receiving your inquiry.

01
You write to us

A brief description of the project, its location, and when you intend to begin. No decks required.

02
We respond in three days

If the project is a possible fit, we will write back with a few questions and propose a call.

03
A first meeting

An hour-long conversation — in person in Delhi or Bengaluru, or by video call. No charge and no obligation.

04
A formal proposal

If we are aligned, we present a scope and fee proposal within two weeks of the initial meeting.
